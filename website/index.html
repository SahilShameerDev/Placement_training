Welcome to Toc H

Established in 2002, Toc H Institute of Science and Technology stands as a premier engineering institution in Kerala. With dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, we provide an exceptional learning environment for aspiring engineers and management professionals. Our commitment to academic excellence and innovation has made us a preferred destination for quality education.

Academic Excellence

TIST offers a diverse range of programs including B.Tech in various engineering disciplines, M.Tech specializations, and MBA. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to prepare students for industry challenges. With a focus on research and innovation, we maintain high academic standards recognized by leading accreditation bodies.

Placement & Opportunities

Our robust placement cell has established partnerships with leading national and multinational companies. We take pride in our excellent placement record, with students securing positions in top organizations across sectors. Beyond placements, we focus on overall career development through workshops, internships, and industry-oriented training programs.
